Gulab Jammun

Ingredients

  • 50 gm paneer
  • 2 cup canola oil/ rapeseed oil
  • 500 ml boiling water
  • 1 slice lemon
  • 2 cup powdered sugar
  • 11/2 teaspoon powdered green cardamom
  • 150 gm mashed khoya

Recipe

  1. Prepare the sugar syrup.
  2. Make the dough for Gulab Jamun.
  3. Shape the dough in small balls.
  4. Soak the fried balls in Gulab Jamun

Soan Papdi

Ingredients

  • 11/4 cup Gram flour (besan)
  • 250 gm ghee
  • 11/2 cup Water
  • 1 teaspoon crushed lightly green cardamom
  • 11/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 21/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk

Recipe

  1. Sift the flours together.
  2. Add flour mixture to the pan and roast on low flame.
  3. Prepare syrup simultaneously..
  4. Pour into a greased surface or thali and roll to 1inch thickness lightly
